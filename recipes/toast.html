Toast

We love toast, you can have it for breakfast, lunch and dinner. With sweet or savoury toppings!

Ingredients

  • Toast
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Place toast in toaster
  2. Set timer for 4 minutes, we want golden brown
  3. Remove toast and place butter (non negotiable)
  4. Add topping of choice, I recommend Nutella, Peanut Butter, Hummous, Jam
