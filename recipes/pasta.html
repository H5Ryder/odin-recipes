Pasta

This is a tasty tomota and olive pasta dish. This is a great meal if you are looking for a light but delicious meal for the summer. The best part is it only takes 15 minutes to make!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Olives (piped)
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Chilly
  • Cherry Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Put the pasta in boiling water. Start a 15 minute timer
  2. Chop the garlic thinly
  3. Heat up a pan with oil and add the chopped garlic
  4. After a few minutes, the garlic should be nice and golden, now add the tomatoes and olives
  5. Once 10 minutes have gone by add the tomato sauce to the pan and stir it in
  6. Add the pasta to the pan and sprinkle some salt & pepper
  7. You are now ready to serve!
